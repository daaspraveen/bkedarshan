Our Tourism

Explore the various pilgrimage tours and spiritual journeys we offer

Spiritual Tours

Immerse yourself in the spiritual essence of India with tours to sacred places, temples, and religious sites.

Jyotirlinga Tours

Visit the twelve sacred Jyotirlinga temples dedicated to Lord Shiva, experiencing divine blessings and spiritual awakening.

Char Dhan Yatra

Embark on the holy pilgrimage to the four sacred shrines of Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath in the Himalayas.

Buddhist Circuit Tours

Explore the sacred Buddhist sites across India, tracing the footsteps of Lord Buddha and discovering ancient monasteries and stupas.

Kailash Mansarovar Yatra

Embark on a journey to the mythical Mount Kailash and the sacred Lake Mansarovar, a pilgrimage revered by Hindus, Buddhists, Jains, and Bonpos.

Vaishno Devi Yatra

Pay homage to the Mother Goddess at the holy shrine of Vaishno Devi nestled in the Trikuta Mountains of Jammu and Kashmir.

Rishikesh Meditation Retreats

Immerse yourself in the serene atmosphere of Rishikesh, known as the Yoga Capital of the World, with guided meditation retreats along the banks of the sacred Ganges River.

Golden Temple Pilgrimage

Experience the divine tranquility of the Golden Temple in Amritsar, Punjab, one of the holiest Sikh shrines in the world, and participate in the community kitchen serving free meals to all visitors.

Hemkund Sahib Trek

Embark on a challenging trek to Hemkund Sahib, a Sikh pilgrimage site nestled amidst the picturesque Himalayan mountains, and pay homage at the pristine lake and gurdwara.
